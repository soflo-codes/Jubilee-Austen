A little bit about me.

My journey into tech came as a bit of a surprise. It was the summer after completing my Art History Degree at the University of British Columbia Okanagan. I found myself not sure of what to do next, as much as I loved learning about the history of art, I didnt quite have a skill I wanted to put to use. It wasnt until a friend who is a Software Developer told me to check out Web Development. A month later I was enrolled in Skillcrushs Break into Tech course and I immediately fell in love.

It wasnt until I coded up my very first website that I felt more accomplished than I have in years. Learning a completely new language and skill empowered me beyond words. I have been able to apply my organizational skills to create clean and elegant code. My problem solving skills are essential when learning new skills and mitigating any issues that arise. I have been able to utilize my learning skills to stay focused and efficient when learning different languages. I currently live in the beautiful mountains of Whistler, BC. When I'm not coding I'm outdoors skiing, biking or playing with my dogs.
